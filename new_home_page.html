Student Visa Credibility Interview Quality Sheet

SECTION 1. PRE-INTERVIEW RISK ASSESSMENT

In order to ensure students have the appropriate preparation for their Student visa credibility interview, a pre-interview risk assessment is required for all applicants.

The applicant is not usually required to take part in this stage of the process â€“ the information required should be supplied as part of the completed application.

Any applicant for whom one or more of the criteria in section 1.1 apply should undertake the extended interview. The criteria in which they have been identified as a higher risk of visa refusal are particularly important areas for extended questioning.
